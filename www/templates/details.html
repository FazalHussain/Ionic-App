Welcome

Welcome to KBHH Yard Master. This app will allow us to perform a biosecurity risk assessment of your yard based on responses you provide to questions regarding the husbandry and management of the horses on your yard. There are a series of questions which are grouped into topics which cover the management of resident horses and new arrivals, vaccinations, worming, isolation facilities and infection control. Once completed, a report will be generated containing some of the recommendations to help protect your yard against disease. This will be emailed to you with a suggested time period for reassessment.
